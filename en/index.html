About us

Alfobre is a nursery of projects devoted to the creation of digital strategies and products in the Heritage field. Working with our partners and customers, we create solutions for Heritage documentation, dissemination and mediation.

Digital

To use and develop digital strategies and technologies that facilitate access, participation, reuse and collaboration.

Heritage

To mediate and disseminate the local heritage, material and immaterial, aggregating diverse perspectives and contributions.

Community

To involve and empower publics and communities in discovering, promoting, safeguarding and enjoying the local Heritage.

Digital services and products for documentation, dissemination and mediation of Heritage.
