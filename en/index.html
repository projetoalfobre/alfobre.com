1. nursery for hydrangea plants to transplant

2. figurative place where a large number of things or beings of a certain type are created, produced or developed

About us

Alfobre is a project incubator focused on crafting strategies and digital products in the Cultural Heritage field.

Working with our partners and customers, we design solutions for Heritage documentation, dissemination and mediation based on Free and Open Source Software.

Digital

Using and developing digital strategies and technologies that facilitate access, participation, reuse, and collaboration.

Heritage

Mediating and disseminating Heritage, both material and intangible, by integrating diverse perspectives and contributions.

Community

Engaging and empowering communities in the discovery, revitalization, preservation, and enjoyment of Heritage.

Services e products

3D Models
close

3D Models

Creation of 3D models of objects and sites or places, including archaeological sites or monuments. Models can be original, replicas or reconstructions of non-existent or partially existent specimens.

We use various techniques, such as photogrammetry scanning, polygonal modeling and digital sculpting, adapting the services to the proposed projects.

Virtual Tours

Development of mediated and immersive visits to archaeological sites, monuments, or museums, through navigation among 360º photographs, including aerial views, with interactive exploration of multimedia content (texts, photographs, infographics, illustrations, 3D models, video, audio, games, etc.).

Cartography & GIS

Map making for printing or web and GIS (Geographic Information Systems) services, including digitizing, geocoding, georeference and spatial analysis.

Training

Photogrammetry
close

Photogrammetry

Training in the use of Free and Open Source Software for close-range photogrammetry, including planning, photogrammetric processing, and post-processing.

Training courses and workshops can be introductory and generalist or have a specialized focus (e.g., drone photogrammetry, photogrammetry with low-cost devices, post-processing and optimization of models, etc.).

Get in touch with us and let's design a tailored training course or workshop.

3D Imaging
close

3D Imaging

Training in the use of Free and Open Source Software for 3D modeling and animation. Both areas can be considered separately or integrated as modules within a broader course.

Training courses and workshops can be introductory and generalist or have a specialized focus (e.g., 3D modeling for 3D printing, optimizing 3D models for games, VR or AR, etc.).
