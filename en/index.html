About us

Alfobre is a project incubator focused on crafting strategies and digital products in the Cultural Heritage field.

Working with our partners and customers, we design solutions for Heritage documentation, dissemination and mediation based on Free and Open Source Software.

Digital

Using and developing digital strategies and technologies that facilitate access, participation, reuse, and collaboration.

Heritage

Mediating and disseminating Heritage, both material and intangible, by integrating diverse perspectives and contributions.

Community

Engaging and empowering communities in the discovery, revitalization, preservation, and enjoyment of Heritage.

3D Models

Creation of 3D models of objects, archaeological sites, or monuments, including recreation or reconstruction of non-existent or partially existent specimens, through photogrammetry scanning and 3D modeling.

Virtual Tours
close

Virtual Tours

Mediated and immersive visits to archaeological sites, monuments, or museums, through navigation among 360º photographs, including aerial views, with interactive exploration of multimedia content (texts, photographs, infographics, illustrations, 3D models, video, audio, games, etc.).

With seamless integration into existing websites, visits can be accessed via smartphones, tablets, laptops, desktops, or VR goggles, without the need for additional software.

Click here to contact us about this service.

Cartography & GIS
close

Cartography & GIS

Cartography and GIS services emerge from the intersection of creativity, technology, and data visualization.

Tailored to the presented challenges and needs, we craft thematic and topographic maps for printing, web, and mobile platforms, ensuring they are interactive and animated.

Click here to contact us about this service.

Animation
close

Animation

Animation is a powerful tool for storytelling. Using 2D and 3D animation, with realistic, stylized, more technical, or more artistic visuals, we seek creative solutions that celebrate cultural heritage.

These services include, among other possibilities, the production of virtual reconstructions of objects, ancient structures, or archaeological sites, as well as the creation of educational videos, or animated infographics that present complex concepts in an accessible and engaging way.

Click here to contact us about this service.

Virtual Reality & Augmented Reality
close

Virtual Reality & Augmented Reality

Design and development of immersive and interactive Virtual Reality (VR) and Augmented Reality (AR) experiences that promote engagement and interaction with Cultural Heritage.

The developed solutions always seek the highest possible involvement, dissemination, and longevity. We value the use of Free and Open Source Software, performance on low-cost devices, and accessibility across all types of devices (smartphones, tablets, laptops, desktops, or VR headsets).

Tailor-made projects

Photogrammetry

Photogrammetry

Training in the use of Free and Open Source Software for close-range photogrammetry, including planning, photogrammetric processing, and post-processing.

Training courses and workshops can be introductory and generalist or have a specialized focus (e.g., drone photogrammetry, photogrammetry with low-cost devices, post-processing and optimization of models, etc.).

Get in touch with us and let's design a tailored training course or workshop.
