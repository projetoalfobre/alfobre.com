About us

Alfobre is a project incubator focused on crafting strategies and digital products in the Cultural Heritage field.

Working with our partners and customers, we design solutions for Heritage documentation, dissemination and mediation based on Free and Open Source Software.

Digital

Using and developing digital strategies and technologies that facilitate access, participation, reuse, and collaboration.

Heritage

Mediating and disseminating Heritage, both material and intangible, by integrating diverse perspectives and contributions.

Community

Engaging and empowering communities in the discovery, revitalization, preservation, and enjoyment of Heritage.

3D Models

Creation of 3D models of objects, sites, and spaces — including archaeological sites and monuments. These models can be original works, faithful replicas, or reconstructions of pieces that are lost or only partially preserved.

We employ a range of techniques, such as photogrammetry-based scanning, polygonal modeling, and digital sculpting, tailoring each project to its specific needs and goals.

Virtual Tours

Development of guided and immersive visit experiences to archaeological sites, monuments, or museums, combining navigation through 360º photographs — including aerial views — with the interactive exploration of multimedia content such as texts, photographs, infographics, illustrations, 3D models, videos, audio, games, and more.

Cartography & GIS

Creation of maps for print or web, as well as GIS (Geographic Information Systems) services, including digitization, geocoding, georeferencing, and spatial analysis.

We offer fully customized services for the creation, collection, processing, and organization of geodata, tailored to the specific needs and preferences of each project, with a special focus on open data sources — such as OpenStreetMap — as well as public agencies and the private sector.

Photogrammetry

Training on the use of Free and Open Source Software for close-range photogrammetry, covering planning, image capture, photogrammetric processing, and post-processing.

Training sessions can be introductory and generalist or specialized, focusing on areas such as drone photogrammetry, use of low-cost devices, post-processing, and model optimization, among others.

3D Imaging
close

3D Imaging

Training on the use of Free and Open Source Software for 3D modeling and animation. These areas can be covered separately or combined as modules within a broader course.

Training sessions can be introductory and generalist or specialized, focusing on topics such as editing 3D models obtained through photogrammetry, optimization for 3D printing, or for Virtual and Augmented Reality, among others.
